Neural network used in: https://benanne.github.io/2014/08/05/spotify-cnns.html

Technical Specifics

  • Dataset: 1 million 30-second excerpts taken from the middle of Spotify’s most popular tracks. 0.5 million for training, 5000 for online validation, & rest for testing
  • Type of network (just an example): Convolutional NN (designed for audio signals rather than images): 4 convolutional layers 3 dense layers
  • Input: Mel-spectrograms obtained from audio signals - (599, 128)
  • Output: Predicts 40 latent factors taken from Spotify’s collaborative filtering models - (40, 1)
