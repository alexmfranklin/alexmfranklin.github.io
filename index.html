Generating Elements of Music Genres

Project Description

Neural network used in: https://benanne.github.io/2014/08/05/spotify-cnns.html Dataset: 1 million 30-second excerpts taken from the middle of Spotify’s most popular tracks. 0.5 million for training 5000 for online validation Rest for testing Type of network (just an example): Convolutional NN (designed for audio signals rather than images) 4 convolutional layers 3 dense layers Input: Mel-spectrograms obtained from audio signals (599, 128) Output: Predicts 40 latent factors taken from Spotify’s collaborative filtering models (40, 1)

Project Goals

  1. Learn how to interpret music as data
  2. Create computer generated music files
  3. Observe musical patterns the neural network learns to generate
