Generating Elements of Music Genres

Generating the Essence of Music Genres

Project Description

I want to explore how neural networks can be used to generate music. The project will take particular genre(s) of music and train a neural network to generate its own music files that hopefully captures "elements" of the genre. By elements of the genre I mean melodic patterns, harmonies, textures, etc that appear in different genres of music that make them recognizable as being in that genre. The idea isn't necessarily to emulate real compositions or have the music sound "human-like" but just to see of the generated music is somewhat recognizable as being in that genre. This could provide a new (non-rigourous) angle for analysing music by observing what patterns the neural network learns.

Project Goals

  1. Learn how to interpret music as data
  2. Create computer generated music files
  3. Observe musical patterns the neural network learns to generate
